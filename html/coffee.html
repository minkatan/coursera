Sharing Coffee Journey

March 2022 by Min Ka

Sharing a little bit of me on Coffee everyone should know. And must have in coffee lovers kitchen below.

  • Moka Pot - nothing brew dark roast coffee better than the moka pot.
  • Coffee Grinder - coffee taste better after smelling freshly blend coffee. Seriously, you can control how fine or coarse you want to be and that makes a huge different in flavour.
  • A Decent Drip Coffee. Spend >$150, you will thank me later.
Light Roasts

Bring the best out of coffee as a fruit. Coffee taste different in different serving temperature, stil taste great even it turns cold.

Find it best to brew it with Pour Over method.

Medium Roasts

Best type of roasts in all season. I drink medium roasts on daily basis.

Great regardless the brewing method.

Dark Roasts

Most coffee drinker I know prefer dark roasts, it has the strongest "coffee" taste. (means burnt)

Best with moka pot or any range of espresso coffee
